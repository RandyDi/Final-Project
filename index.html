Welcome!

This website was created for those who just need to look at pictures of Animals in order to just forget about the rainy day.

Cat Page: To interact with the page just press on a generated fact to watch it fade out of the screen. To generate a new picture alongside new facts just press the button

Dog Page: To interact with the page press on any dog photo to watch it fade out of the screen. At all times there will be five dog phtots available. To change between only Shibe and all dogs just press the button and all photos will change to the button you have pressed. You are also able to search for dogs by breed

If you are looking for a sub-breed dog, put the breed name first then sub-breed in order for the search to work (E.g. hound blood, hound afghan, bulldog french)
